Gardevoir ex - SV01 086/198

While Gardevoir ex is the most important card in the Gardevoir deck, it's more for it's ability rather than the attack. 190 damage is a fairly good amount of damage to deal, but the ability can power up other Pokemon that can do signicantly more damage. Gardevoir ex is a solid attacker though as it has a lot of HP.

Gardevoir - SWSH06 061/198

Gardevoir has been out for over a year, but it wasn't good until Gardevoir ex came out. It has a very good ability that can help accelerate some energy, but at the worst draw you 2 cards! It's also one of the main attackers, but needs some additional setup to hit extremely high numbers.

Kirlia - SWSH12 068/195

Kirlia is an extremely valuable setup Pokemon. It's ability allows you to draw so many cards while throwing away energies and useless cards. Being a stage 2 deck, having such a powerful draw engine makes it much more consistent and faster at setting up.

Ralts - SWSH10 060/189

Ralts is purely a setup card. Without it, you cannot evolve into the powerful evolutions of Kirlia or Gardevoir. Sometimes it's attack can help set up math on Pokemon with just a little too much HP.

Zacian V - CLB 016/025

Zacian V is a very strong card in the Gardevoir deck. It's ability helps get energy out early without damaging your Pokemon. In the later game, it's a powerful attacker that can one-hit knockout almost everything in the game.
